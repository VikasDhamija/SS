Business Profile

At Soup Square, we specialize in delivering delicious, homemade soups straight to your doorstep. Our soups are made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and crafted with love to warm your heart and soul. Whether you're craving a classic tomato soup or vegitable quinova soup, we've got you covered.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide you wholesome, comforting soups that bring joy and nourishment to their day. We believe in the power of good food to bring people together and strive to make every bowl of soup a delightful experience.
